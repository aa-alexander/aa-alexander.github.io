About me

I'm Arun Alexander Anburaj (அருண் அலெக்சாண்டர் அன்புராஜ்) from Tamilnadu, India. I'm a PhD student in the Institute of Microbiology at Heinrich-Heine-Universität Düsseldorf.

I'm interested in studying host-microbes interactions. For my doctoral studies I'm studying the cross kingdom RNA transport between pathogenic fungi and plants through extracellular vesicles.
