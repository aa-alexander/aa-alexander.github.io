About me

I'm Arun Alexander Anburaj (அருண் அலெக்சாண்டர் அன்புராஜ்) from Tamilnadu, India. I majored in plant sciences for both my undergraduate and postgraduate degree.

My interest in fungal biology biology began during my undergraduate degree when I was working on a short-term research project on endophytic fungi funded from Loyola Research Park. Endophytes piqued my interest in 'how a pathogen in one plant can be a beneficial endophyte in another', as well as 'how an endophyte can produce the same bioactive compounds as its plant host' and 'are endophytes truly mutualistic or opportunistic microbe adaptation'. To answer these research questions I pursued my education in plant sciences and decided to work on my reserach questions in my future.
